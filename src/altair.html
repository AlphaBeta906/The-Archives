Altair, formerly Alexis Nier Jackson (c. 1754 - December 5th, 1849) was a Perusian writer, polymath and father of event prediction. He was the founder of the Great Society of the Archivists, and the first General Overseer of the Archivists.

Born circa 1754 at Antwain, Perusia, he studied at Antronia, Alphadonia. Circa 1775, he was transported to the Hub of Worlds, and met Nayáquille, Patron God of Learning and Knowledge, and God of Wisdom, Creativity and Arts. He experienced an epiphany and realized the potential of event prediction.
